Everybody's 2 favourite flavours combined

Dale Earnhardt Jr swears it's delicious

Ingredients

    Your choice of shitty bread, any shitty bread will do

    3/4 of a full banana, make sure one end is chopped straight

    Half a litre of Hellmanns mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Lay the bread flat

  2. Slice off 1/4 of the banana and throw it in the bin

  3. Slather around half a litre of the mayonnaise onto your bread slices

  4. Insert banana

  5. Close sandwich and prepare for ultimate satisfaction
