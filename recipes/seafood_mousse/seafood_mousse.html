Seafood Mousse

Have you been looking for grandma's old seafood mousse recipe?

Well, you're in luck...

Ingredients

  • 1 envelope unflavored gelatin

  • 1/4 cup cold water

  • 1/2 cup boiling water

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise

  • 1 tablespoon lime juice

  • 1 tablespoon grated onion

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

  • 2 cups fish (halibut, salmon or tuna), drained and finely chopped

  • 1 tablespoon capers, chopped

  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

  • 1/8 teaspoon dried tarragon

  • 1/8 teaspoon dried crushed marjoram

  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

  • Carrot strips and sliced olives (optional)

Steps

  1. Soften gelatin in cold water

  2. Add boiling water and stir until it dissolves

  3. Let cool

  4. Add mayonnaise, lime juice, onion, salt and pepper, mix well and chill until mixture begins to set

  5. Fold in fish, capers, parsley, tarragon, marjoram and whipped cream.

  6. Pour into an oiled fish mold and chill until firm.

  7. Unmold on serving platter and if desired garnish with carrot strips and stuffed olive slices
